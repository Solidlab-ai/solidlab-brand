# Solidlab Content Templates

20 ferdige templates du kan bruke direkte eller tilpasse.

## 📱 LinkedIn-poster

### Template 1: Produkt-milestone
```
[Produkt] passerte [milestone] denne uken.

[2-3 konkrete detaljer — tall, brukerfeedback, teknisk highlight]

[Lærdom eller observasjon]

/ [valgt tagline]

#[3-5 hashtags]
```

### Template 2: Behind-the-scenes bygg
```
Solidlab bygget [produkt] fordi [problemformulering].

[Tidslinjen — "fire måneder fra første linje kode til X"]

[Teknisk detalj — stack, utfordring løst]

[Resultat i tall]

Bygget i Stavanger. [Konkret bruks-anekdote].

/ products, ventures, sometimes chaos
```

### Template 3: Lesson learned
```
[Ting du prøvde] fungerte ikke. Her er hva vi lærte.

[2-3 korte punkter om hva som gikk galt]

[Hva vi endret til]

[Ny resultat]

Bygger-hverdagen er ikke lineær. Men den er konkret.
```

### Template 4: Partnerskap/kunde
```
[Kundenavn] begynte å bruke [produkt/tjeneste] i [periode].

[Konkret resultat for dem]

[En lærdom fra samarbeidet]

Takk til [navn] for å la oss bygge sammen.
```

### Template 5: Team/kultur
```
[Observasjon om team-dynamikk]

[Konkret eksempel — ikke generisk "teamwork"-prat]

[Lærdom]

Små team, lange horisonter.
```

```
Hilsen,
[navn]
```

## 🎨 Bildeprompter (til Gemini/Midjourney/openart)

### Template 17: Founder portrait
```
Photorealistic professional portrait of a Nordic entrepreneur in 
his early 30s, clean-shaven, natural north-facing window light, 
wearing a plain charcoal merino sweater, sitting at a minimalist 
oak desk with a laptop, shelf of architecture books behind him 
slightly out of focus. 

Color palette: warm off-white #F4F1EA, deep charcoal #0A0A0A, 
steel blue #4A5A62 accents. 

Kinfolk magazine aesthetic. Shot on Fujifilm X100V at f/2.8. 
No smile, contemplative expression. Natural skin texture. 
16:9 aspect ratio.
```

### Template 18: Product flat-lay
```
Top-down flat lay photography on warm off-white linen background: 
[list objects — e.g., "a cream business card with solidlab.ai 
wordmark, a black round sticker with /s symbol, a small brass pen, 
a sprig of dried lavender"]. 

Soft natural light from the left. Minimal composition with 70% 
negative space. 

Color palette: warm off-white #F4F1EA, deep charcoal #0A0A0A, 
steel blue accents. 

Kinfolk magazine aesthetic. 1:1 square format for Instagram.
```

### Template 19: Landscape/mood
```
Nordic coastal landscape photography at golden hour. Single wooden 
cabin on rocky shore, warm interior light glowing through one window. 
Calm fjord water in foreground. Low contrast, muted palette of 
warm off-white, charcoal, and steel blue. 

Shot on medium format film, natural grain, atmospheric fog. 
Minimalist composition with 70% negative space. 
Wallpaper magazine editorial aesthetic. 

16:9 widescreen.
```

### Template 20: Isometric brand illustration
```
Minimalist isometric illustration of [scene — e.g., "a small Nordic 
laboratory workspace with three floating elements: a laptop with code, 
a coffee mug, and a sailboat in the distance"].

Strict color palette: warm off-white background #F4F1EA, deep 
charcoal #0A0A0A, steel blue #4A5A62.

Thin 1px line work, flat shading, no gradients. 
Add small label "solidlab.ai" in sans-serif font. 
Professional Nordic brand illustration style. 
1:1 square format.
```
