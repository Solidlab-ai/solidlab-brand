/ 05 — voice

How we write. And how we don't.

/ good ✓
"We shipped Bølgevarsel in 2026."
Specific. Named. Dated. Says what we did, for whom, and when. Precision builds trust.
/ bad ✗
"Leveraging cutting-edge AI to revolutionize the future."
Jargon. Vague. Promises revolution instead of results. Says nothing we actually do.
